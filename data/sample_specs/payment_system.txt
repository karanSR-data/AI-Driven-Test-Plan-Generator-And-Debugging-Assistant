Payment System Requirements

1. Users can pay using Visa and Mastercard.

2. Payment transactions must complete within 30 seconds.

3. Failed payments shall display an appropriate error message.

4. Users shall receive payment confirmation via email.

5. Refund requests can be raised within 7 days.

6. The system shall prevent duplicate transactions.

7. Payment details must be encrypted.

8. Transaction logs shall be maintained for auditing purposes.
