Login System Requirements

1. Users shall log in using email and password.

2. Password length must be between 8 and 16 characters.

3. The system shall lock the account after 5 consecutive failed login attempts.

4. Users can reset their password using the Forgot Password feature.

5. The system shall display an error message for invalid credentials.

6. Session timeout shall occur after 15 minutes of inactivity.

7. User passwords must be stored in encrypted form.

8. The login page shall support Chrome, Firefox, and Edge browsers.

9. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) shall be supported.

10. Successful login shall redirect the user to the dashboard.
